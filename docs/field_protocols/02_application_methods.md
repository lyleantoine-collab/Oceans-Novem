# Field Application Methods – Ocean’s Novem Granules

| Method              | Dose                  | Equipment needed               | Best for                     | Real-world example |
|---------------------|-----------------------|--------------------------------|------------------------------|--------------------|
| Surface broadcast   | 0.5–2 % w/w soil      | Tractor spreader or backpack   | Large open sites             | Alberta Fixed Earth pilots |
| Soil mixing         | 1–3 % in top 30 cm    | Rototiller or excavator        | Hotspots, gardens            | Wisconsin 97 % pilot |
| Injection (groundwater) | 0.1–0.5 g/L       | Direct-push or wells           | Plumes                       | Regenesis Ontario sites |
| Spray slurry        | 5–10 % suspension    | Hydroseeder                    | Sloped or frozen ground      | Newfoundland winter ready |
