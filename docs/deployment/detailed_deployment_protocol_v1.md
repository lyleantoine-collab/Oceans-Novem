# Detailed Deployment Protocol – Ocean’s Novem v1.0 (November 2025)

KISS: Brew → Test → Deploy → Monitor → Verify. For 1–10,000 m² sites. All steps CL1 biosafety.

## Phase 1: Site Prep (Days 1–7)
| Step | Action | Tools/Inputs | Responsible | Checkpoint |
|------|--------|--------------|-------------|------------|
| 1.1  | Soil/water baseline: Sample 10 points (0–30 cm depth), test ∑PFAS (LC-MS/MS), pH, TOC | Grab sampler, lab kit ($500/site) | Site lead | <70 µg/kg PFAS trigger? Proceed. |
| 1.2  | Risk screen: Check for co-contams (arsenic via ICP-MS), slope (>15%? Add geotextile) | GIS map, AGQS limits | Engineer | FPIC signed if Indigenous land. |
| 1.3  | Weather hold: No deploy if <5°C or >50 mm rain/24h | Local forecast | All | Delay 48h if needed. |

## Phase 2: Granule Application (Days 8–10)
| Method | Site Type | Dose (kg/ha) | Equipment | Post-Apply |
|--------|-----------|--------------|-----------|------------|
| Broadcast | Flat/open (>1 ha) | 10–20 | Tractor spreader | Rake 5 cm, mulch 2 cm straw. |
| Injection | Groundwater plume | 5–10 (g/L) | Direct-push rig | Seal wells, add 5% molasses booster. |
| Slurry Spray | Sloped/frozen | 15–25 (5% susp.) | Hydroseeder | Tackifier (0.5 kg/ha) for erosion control. |

## Phase 3: Activation & Monitoring (Months 1–12)
| Month | Metrics | Method | Threshold | Action if Fail |
|-------|---------|--------|-----------|----------------|
| 1     | Microbial survival (>10^6 CFU/g) | qPCR (16S) | All 9 strains present | Re-dose 50%. |
| 3     | PFAS drop (>30%) + F- rise (>20%) | LC-MS/MS + IC | Chain-specific | Amend pH to 6.8. |
| 6     | Degradation (>60%) | 19F-NMR | Mineralization >90% | Add nutrient pulse. |
| 12    | Full (>90%) + toxicity (Microtox < baseline) | Full suite | <4 ppt | Closeout report. |

Cost/site (1 ha): $5k–15k (granules $2k, labor $3k, tests $5k–10k).  
Safety: Gloves, autoclave waste. Emergency: Lime spill (pH >9 kills in 2h).
