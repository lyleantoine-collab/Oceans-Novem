# Troubleshooting Ocean’s Novem – PhD-tested fixes

| Problem                          | Likely cause                     | Fix                                      |
|----------------------------------|----------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| No growth after 1 week           | Wrong pH (<5.5 or >8.5)          | Buffer to 6.8–7.2 with lime or sulfur   |
| Smells like rotten eggs          | Too anaerobic → sulfate reducers dominating | Add light aeration or Methylocystis    |
| Granules not sticking to soil    | Too dry                          | Pre-wet site or use 5 % molasses spray  |
| Fluoride levels not rising       | Defluorination stalled           | Check temperature (needs >10 °C)        |
| White mold everywhere            | Phanerochaete took over          | Reduce wood carrier from 50→20 %        |
