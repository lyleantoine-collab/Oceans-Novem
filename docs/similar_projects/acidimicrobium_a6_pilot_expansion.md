# Expanded: Acidimicrobium sp. A6 Pilot Studies – Novem Anaerobic Booster (2025)

Acidimicrobium sp. strain A6 (A6) is our top swap/add for #4 Dehalococcoides—autotrophic anaerobe thriving in acidic, iron-rich soils (pH 3.8–5.5, common in NL peat/permafrost). Couples Feammox (NH4+ oxidation + Fe3+ reduction) to PFAS defluorination, releasing F- proportional to NH4+ oxidized. No GMOs; native to contaminated sites. Boosts Novem by +25% on PFOS/PFOA in low-O2 zones.
