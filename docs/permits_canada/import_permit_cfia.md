# Step 1 – Importing the Bugs into Canada (2025)

1. Go here: https://inspection.canada.ca → “Import Permit”  
2. Fill form for “Terrestrial Animal Pathogens” → Directive D-12-02  
3. Say: “Risk Group 1 environmental microorganisms for research”  
4. Cost = FREE  
5. Takes 2–4 weeks  
6. All our 9 strains qualify → no problems ever reported.

Link to blank form: https://inspection.canada.ca/importing-food-plants-or-animals/terrestrial-animals-import-policies/directive-d-12-02/eng/...
